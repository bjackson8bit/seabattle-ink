Splatoon Tournaments and Community

Events

SeaBATTLE! is Seattle's premier Splatoon LAN!
We run tournaments 4 times per year, and typically brings in around 10 teams and 50 attendees. Our events are generally at Gameworks in Downtown Seattle, but every spring we run SeaBATTLE! Royale, our largest scale event at University of Washington!

Community

Our community has been growing and we've even had attendees from as far as Europe! Local players bring their friends from all over to compete and hang out at our events. You can find our socials and community servers below.
Any level of experience is welcome, and if you're on your own, we can even pair you with a team for events! All we ask is that you bring an open mind and are accepting of others.
Happy Splatting!
